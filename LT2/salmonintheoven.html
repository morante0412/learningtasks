Salmon in the Oven

Ingredients

  • 1 fillet of salmon, skinned (tails are cheapest)
  • Any veggies you like: tomatoe, peppers, zucchinis, etc
  • Liquid cream
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Method

  1. 1) 1. Put the salmon in an oven dish - add chopped veggies and cream on top - with salt and pepper
  2. 2. Grill on a high heat for about 25 min. If it is a thick fillet, it will take longer to cook through, always check if the middle if cooked.
  3. 3. You can serve it with rice or pasta
